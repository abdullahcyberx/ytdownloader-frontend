Frequently Asked Questions

Is it legal to download videos?

Downloading videos for personal, offline viewing is generally acceptable under fair use. However, you should not re-distribute copyrighted material for commercial gain.

Why isn't my video downloading in 4K or 1080p?

Our tool fetches the maximum quality available from the source metadata. If the creator originally uploaded the video in 720p, our system cannot artificially upscale it to 1080p.

Does this work natively on iOS and Android?

Absolutely. Our platform is built as a Progressive Web Application. You can access it via Safari or Chrome on any mobile device and save videos directly to your camera roll.
